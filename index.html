⭐⭐
  • 9:00出发去感通索道Head to Gantong Cableway
  • 9:30-12:00缆车上山(2600m) → 走玉带路平缓栈道(像公园散步)Cable car up (2600m) → Jade Belt Path (flat, easy walk)

  • 下午休整，老人午休Rest, nap for elderly
  • 晚上古城溜达（步行即到）Stroll ancient town (walking distance)
为什么选感通索道？
✅ 封闭式缆车，安全感强
✅ 到达2600m（非高海拔），老人友好
✅ 玉带路=山腰平路，不爬山，风景绝美
❌ 不选洗马潭索道（3920m，老人易高反）
Why Gantong Cableway?
✅ Enclosed cable car
✅ 2600m only — elderly-friendly
✅ Jade Belt Path = flat mountain trail, no climbing
❌ NOT Ximatan (3920m — altitude risk for elderly)

🌙 每晚 · 古城+篝火

🌙 Evenings · Old Town + Bonfire

住苍洱大道，步行到古城几分钟。任何一天晚上都能去。

Cang'er Avenue → Old Town is a few minutes' walk. Any evening works.


篝火晚会怎么找：

  • 入住后问酒店前台——最靠谱
  • 人民路一些酒吧/餐吧晚上会点篝火
  • 洱海门附近有时有自发篝火打跳
  • 小红书搜"大理 篝火晚会 2026年7月"

Finding bonfire events:

  • Ask hotel front desk — most reliable
  • Some bars on Renmin Road light bonfires at night
  • Erhai Gate area sometimes has spontaneous gatherings
  • Search RED (Xiaohongshu): "大理 篝火晚会 July 2026"
